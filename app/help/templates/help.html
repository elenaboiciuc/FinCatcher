Getting Started

Creating an account is easy! Simply click on the "Register/Login" button on the homepage, enter your details, and follow the instructions. Once registered, you can log in using your username and password.

Navigation

The main menu on the left side of the screen provides access to key sections of FinCatcher:

  • Overview
  • Transactions
  • Budgets
  • Goals
  • Categories
  • Export Data

Using the Features

Overview Page

The Overview page provides a snapshot of your financial status, including charts that compare monthly expenses vs. income and the distribution of your top 5 spending categories.

Transactions Page

On the Transactions page, you can add new transactions, edit existing ones, and delete transactions.

Budgets Page

Set up your budgets by clicking on "Create Budget" and entering your income and expense categories. Track your spending against the budgeted amounts to stay on top of your finances.

Goals Page

Set financial goals, such as saving for a vacation or paying off debt. Track your progress to stay motivated and on track. Only transactions that include the goal name in the description will be considered, so please set the descriptions carefully.

Categories Page

Manage your categories by adding new ones, editing existing names, and deleting unused categories. This helps keep your transactions organized.

Export Data

The Export Data page allows you to download your financial data in a CSV. To export your data, simply choose the month and year and press "Export" button.

Common Issues and Troubleshooting

I can't log in

Ensure you are using the correct username and password. If you've forgotten your password, please contact our support team.

I can't see my transactions

Check if you have the correct date range selected and ensure that your transactions are correctly categorized.

The charts are not displaying

Refresh the page and make sure you have a stable internet connection. If the problem persists, please contact support.

Contact Support

If you need further assistance or have any questions, please feel free to reach out to our support team at support@fincatcher.com.
