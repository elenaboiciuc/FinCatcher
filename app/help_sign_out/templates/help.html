Help Center

FinCatcher Concept

"FinCatcher" embodies the concept of actively gathering and managing your financial elements. Much like a dream catcher filters out unwanted dreams while allowing good ones to pass, FinCatcher symbolizes discerning financial decisions—capturing beneficial actions and organizing them harmoniously. It suggests vigilance and intentionality in financial management.

Getting Started

Creating an account is easy! Simply click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, enter your details, and follow the instructions. Once registered, you can log in using your username and password.

Navigation

The main menu on the left side of the screen provides access to key sections of FinCatcher:

  • Overview
  • Transactions
  • Budgets
  • Goals
  • Categories

Using the Features

Overview Page

The Overview page provides a snapshot of your financial status, including charts that compare monthly expenses vs. income and the distribution of your top 5 spending categories.

Transactions Page

On the Transactions page, you can:

  • Add new transactions by clicking the "Add Transaction" button.
  • Edit existing ones by clicking the edit icon.
  • Delete transactions by clicking the delete icon.

Budgets Page

Set up your budgets by clicking on "Create Budget" and entering your income and expense categories. Track your spending against the budgeted amounts to stay on top of your finances.

Categories Page

Manage your categories by:

  • Adding new categories
  • Editing existing names
  • Deleting unused categories
This helps keep your transactions organized.

Goals Page

Set financial goals, such as saving for a vacation or paying off debt. Track your progress to stay motivated and on track.

Common Issues and Troubleshooting

I can't log in

Ensure you are using the correct username and password. If you've forgotten your password, click on "Forgot Password" to reset it.

I can't see my transactions

Check if you have the correct date range selected and ensure that your transactions are correctly categorized.

The charts are not displaying

Refresh the page and make sure you have a stable internet connection. If the problem persists, please contact support.

FAQs

How do I reset my password? Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions.

How do I export my data? Go to the Transactions page and click on "Export" to download your data in CSV format.
